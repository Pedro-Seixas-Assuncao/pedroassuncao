Pig Game Rules

  1. This game needs two players.
  2. The objective is to be the first player to reach or exceed 100 points.
  3. If the players take turns rolling a six-sided they die.
  4. If a player takes a "1", all points accumulated during that round are lost.
  5. After each roll, the players have the option to stop and keep the points accumulated so far.
